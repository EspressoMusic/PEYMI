Privacy Policy

Peymiz (also branded as Bizmi in some builds)

Version: pilot-v1 · Last updated: 20 May 2026

Pilot / beta notice. Peymiz is in a limited pilot. Features, data practices, and this policy may change before a full public or paid launch. This document is a pilot-ready draft, not legal advice.

1. Who we are

Peymiz is an international software platform that helps small businesses create a business page and receive orders, appointments, and customer messages. The operator of the pilot service can be contacted at privacy@peymiz.com.

2. What Peymiz is

Peymiz provides hosting and tools for business owners. Each business is responsible for its own storefront content, products, services, pricing, and customer relationships. Peymiz processes personal data on behalf of business operations and platform administration as described below.

3. Data we collect — business owners

When you register or manage a business, we may collect:

  • Account identifiers (e.g. email, user ID)
  • Profile details (name, phone number when verified)
  • Business information (store name, public link/slug, description, address, phone, logo, store terms you publish)
  • Authentication and security logs (e.g. sign-in events, subject to our infrastructure provider)
  • Records that you accepted our Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy (version and timestamp)

4. Data we collect — customers and visitors

When someone uses a business page or the app as a customer, we may collect:

  • Order details (items, quantities, notes, status)
  • Appointment details (date, time, service, status, optional notes)
  • Contact or message content (name, phone, email when provided, message text)
  • Account identifiers if the person signs in
  • Technical data typical of mobile/web apps (device type, app events needed to run the service)

Business owners can see customer data submitted to their business (orders, appointments, messages). Peymiz does not use this data for unrelated marketing to customers without a separate lawful basis and notice.

5. Why we collect data

  • To provide and operate the platform (accounts, stores, orders, appointments, messaging)
  • To verify business owners (e.g. phone verification before creating a store)
  • To secure the service, prevent abuse, and enforce terms
  • To support pilot operations, troubleshooting, and improvements
  • To comply with applicable law where required

6. How we use data

We use personal data only for purposes consistent with this policy: running Peymiz, displaying business pages, routing orders and appointments to the correct business, communicating about the service, and protecting users. We do not sell personal data.

7. Where data is stored

Data is stored using cloud infrastructure. Our primary database and authentication provider is Supabase (hosted in regions configured for the project). Data may be processed in countries other than your own. We rely on our providers’ security measures and contractual safeguards where applicable.

8. Third-party processors

We use service providers that process data on our instructions, including:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, storage, and serverless functions
  • SMS / phone verification providers (e.g. Twilio) when enabled for OTP
  • Payment processors (e.g. Stripe) when paid features are enabled — only with data needed for payments
  • App distribution platforms (Google Play, Apple App Store) as applicable

We choose providers with appropriate security practices. Their privacy policies also apply to their processing.

9. Data security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures for a pilot SaaS product, including access controls, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security in the database where configured, and avoiding storage of unnecessary secrets in client apps. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; report concerns to privacy@peymiz.com.

10. Retention

We keep data while your account or business is active and as needed for operations, disputes, or legal obligations. Retention periods for a full launch will be documented in a later policy version. You may request deletion subject to section 11 and legal exceptions.

11. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA/CPRA, Israel Privacy Protection Law), you may have rights to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete data where applicable law allows
  • Object or restrict certain processing where applicable
  • Complain to a supervisory authority (e.g. ICO, CPPA, Israeli Privacy Protection Authority)

To exercise rights, email privacy@peymiz.com with enough detail to identify your account. We will respond within reasonable timeframes required by law. We do not claim full compliance with every jurisdiction in this pilot draft; a lawyer review is planned before full public launch.

12. International users

Peymiz is offered to international users. By using the service, you understand that data may be processed in countries where we or our providers operate. We aim to describe our practices clearly and honor applicable rights requests.

13. Children

Peymiz is not directed at children under 16. Do not use the service if you are under the minimum age required in your country without parental consent.

14. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version with an updated date and version ID. Material changes may require renewed acceptance in the app.

15. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@peymiz.com
General support: support@peymiz.com
