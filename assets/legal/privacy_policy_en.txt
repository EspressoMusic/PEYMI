Privacy Policy — Peymiz (Bizmi)
Version: pilot-v1 · Last updated: 20 May 2026

PILOT / BETA NOTICE
Peymiz is in a limited pilot. Features, data practices, and this policy may change before a full public or paid launch. This document is a pilot-ready draft, not legal advice.

1. Who we are
Peymiz is an international software platform for small businesses. Contact: privacy@peymiz.com

2. What Peymiz is
Peymiz helps business owners create a business page and receive orders, appointments, and customer messages. Each business is responsible for its own storefront and customers.

3. Data we collect — business owners
• Account identifiers (email, user ID)
• Profile details (name, verified phone)
• Business information (name, slug, description, address, phone, logo, store terms)
• Security-related logs via our infrastructure
• Terms/Privacy acceptance (version and timestamp)

4. Data we collect — customers and visitors
• Orders (items, notes, status)
• Appointments (date, time, service, notes)
• Messages (name, phone, email if provided, text)
• Account data if signed in
• Technical data needed to run the app

Business owners see data submitted to their business only.

5. Why we collect data
To operate the platform, verify owners, secure the service, support the pilot, and comply with law where required.

6. How we use data
We use data to run Peymiz and protect users. We do NOT sell personal data.

7. Where data is stored
Data is stored in cloud infrastructure, primarily Supabase (region configured for the project). Data may be processed outside your country.

8. Third-party processors
• Supabase (database, auth, storage, functions)
• SMS providers (e.g. Twilio) for phone verification when enabled
• Payment processors (e.g. Stripe) when paid features launch
• App stores as applicable

9. Data security
We use reasonable measures: TLS, access controls, database row-level security where configured, no service-role keys in client apps. No system is perfectly secure.

10. Retention
Data is kept while accounts/businesses are active and as needed for operations or law. Deletion requests: see section 11.

11. Your rights
Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, delete, object, or complain to a regulator (e.g. ICO, CPPA, Israeli PPA). Email privacy@peymiz.com. We do not claim full GDPR/CCPA compliance in this pilot draft; lawyer review is planned before full launch.

12. International users
Data may be processed where we or providers operate. We aim to honor applicable requests.

13. Children
Not directed at children under 16.

14. Changes
We may update this policy; new version and date will be published.

15. Contact
privacy@peymiz.com · support@peymiz.com
